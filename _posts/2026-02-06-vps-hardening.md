They go step-by-step on things such as:
- Provisioning the VPS
- Pointing a domain to the VPS
- Setting up a non-root user for access
- SSH configurations for further secured access
- Firewall setup
- Automated updates and mailer
- Suspicious address banning

This is most poignant guide I found during my dive into this topic, visit Tenner's guide [here](https://danieltenner.com/setting-up-and-hardening-a-hetzner-server/#fnref:8).
